About Manchester United Football Club

Manchester United Football Club is one of the world's most popular and successful football clubs. Founded in 1878 as Newton Heath LYR Football Club, the club changed its name to Manchester United in 1902 and moved to its current home, Old Trafford, in 1910. The club has a rich history of success, having won numerous domestic and international titles, including a record 20 English league titles, 12 FA Cups, and three UEFA Champions League titles. Known for its attacking style of play and iconic red jerseys, Manchester United has a massive global fan base and is renowned for producing some of the greatest football talents in history.
